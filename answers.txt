Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

Answer: To edit and test without messing up current working versions.

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.

Answer: Data science could be applied to commerce. Patterns of consumer habits can be extracted by using data science with purchasing patterns.
Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?

Answer: Merge keeps the history as it took place, rebasing rewrites the history.
